Click the Right Analogue Stick (R3) to Lock-On to the Enemy.
Tap the 'X' Button to Perform a Light Attack.
Light Attacks are Fast, but don't hit Very Hard.
Tap the 'Y' button to Perform a Heavy Attack.
Heavy Attack's Hit alot Harder, but are also alot Slower.
You can perform a Combo by landing consecutive Light/Heavy Attacks.
The Blue bar is Your Power of the Gods Bar. Once this is full, Press the 'B' button to gain a boost in Health, Speed and Damage.
You are now ready to take on the tower. May the Gods Be With You, Incursor.
